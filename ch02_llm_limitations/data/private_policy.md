# ACME On-Call Policy v2026-02-20

## Severity Definitions
- Sev-1: production outage or critical security impact.
- Sev-2: major degradation with workaround.
- Sev-3: minor issue.

## Escalation Path (Sev-1)
- At 0 minutes: page primary on-call.
- At 7 minutes: page backup engineer if not stabilized.
- At 15 minutes: escalate to incident commander.
- At 25 minutes: escalate to director on duty.

## Communication
- Post status every 10 minutes in #incidents.
- Include customer impact, mitigation, and ETA.

## Compensation
- Weeknight on-call stipend: $95/night.
- Weekend on-call stipend: $250/day.
- Sev-1 response bonus: $180 per incident.

## Audit
- Keep timeline and escalation records for 18 months.
